The Team

MVRT prepares its members for a lifetime of success

Team 115 has been competing in the FIRST Robotics Competition for going on 18 years, starting with the 1997 FRC game. We have competed in the annual Silicon Valley Regional and taken the honors at this regional and various other competitions. MVRT has attended the FIRST Championships in St. Louis almost every other year.

In addition, MVRT connects with the community through various outreach activities including but not limited to FLL, ThinkFIRST, and Eduspire.


Who We Are

In 1997, MVRT 115 was founded by eager and enthusiastic students interested in FIRST and its mission. One of the early "first generation teams," MVRT has stood strong for over 15 years as one of the 5000 FRC teams worldwide. MVRT is made up and run by students attending Monta Vista High School.

Our Mission

Dedicated to fostering a passion in science and technology within our community, the Monta Vista Robotics Team inspires students and develops interpersonal skills through team-based engineering challenges.


Important things to remember
  • Students work for 6 weeks on designing and building a robot to take to, and compete in, regional and international competitions
  • Volunteers and mentors from across the community teach and help students with the intensive design process and build season
  • MVRT meetings are not mandatory, but it is important to remember that members who put in more to the team will learn more and have greater opportunity to participate in team activities
